Space Groove

Our Story

In the pursuit of its mission objectives in aeronautics and Space Administration, Space Groove continues pushing the boundaries of its own foundations. Often, new technologies are needed to either enhance our current capabilities or enable future ones. The Interative Partner Program (IPP) provides the organizational structure for acquiring, maturing, technology and capabilities for Space Groove's Mission Programs and Projects through investments and partnerships with Industry, Academia, Government Agencies. As one of Space Groove's Mission Support Offices, the IPP supports all Mission Directorates. In addition to leveraged technology investments, dual-use technology-related partnerships, and accelerates technology maturation.

Job Description

Space Groove is more than astronauts. We are scientists, engineers, IT specialists, technicians working together to break barriers to achieve the seemingly impossible.

We continue Space Groove's legacy of excellence through an array of missions—from laying the foundation to expand our human presence in space to developing technologies to studying Earth’s system.
